Responsive and slick progress bars with animated SVG paths. Use built-\/in shapes or \href{http://progressbarjs.readthedocs.org/en/latest/api/path}{\texttt{ create your own paths}}. \href{http://progressbarjs.readthedocs.org/en/latest/api/parameters\#custom-animations}{\texttt{ Customize}} the animations as you wish.

Documentation is \href{http://progressbarjs.readthedocs.org/en/latest/}{\texttt{ hosted at readthedocs.\+org}}.
